Services.

Everything Your Business Needs — WordPress, WooCommerce & AI-Powered Web Solutions.

ScriptOne is a full-service digital agency building custom WordPress websites, WooCommerce and Magento ecommerce stores, and Agentic AI-powered web applications — including CRM systems, POS platforms, and Medical Portals. One agency, every layer of your digital presence, built to perform.

CUSTOM WORDPRESS WEBSITE DEVELOPMENT

WordPress Websites

Business websites, landing pages, service pages, blogs, and lead generation — built with clean block-based architecture so your team can manage and grow content with ease.

ECOMMERCE STORE DEVELOPMENT

Ecommerce Store

We use WooCommerce and Magento to set up high-converting ecommerce stores — including product catalog, variations, collections, coupons, cart & checkout UX, transactional emails, and end-to-end order management workflows.

AGENTIC AI POWERED CUSTOM WEB APPLICATION DEVELOPMENT

AI-Powered Custom Web Applications

We build intelligent, Agentic AI-powered custom web applications tailored to your business — including CRM systems, POS platforms, Medical Patient Portals, business automation tools, and any bespoke digital solution your operations demand.

DEVOPS · CLOUD · CI/CD · INTEGRATIONS

DevOps, Cloud Infrastructure & Integrations

We deploy and maintain production-grade infrastructure across AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft Azure, DigitalOcean, Heroku, Vercel, and Netlify — containerized with Docker & Kubernetes, automated via GitHub Actions, Terraform, and CI/CD pipelines, and secured with Vault & NGINX.

Development is accelerated using Claude Code and Cursor AI for Agentic AI-assisted engineering. Every project ships with essential third-party integrations — Stripe, PayPal, GetResponse, and other leading platforms — wired cleanly into your application from day one.
